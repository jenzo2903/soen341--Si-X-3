Welcome to our Cat Shelter! Our mission is to provide a safe and loving home for cats in need. Whether

Adoption Requirements for Cats

Main Requirements:

  1. Applicant must be at least 18 years old.
  2. Demonstrate tthe ability to provide proper care, inlcuding food, water, and veterinary care.
  3. Because cats are unique:
    • Ability to interpret mysterious meows is a plus!
    • Acceptance of the occasinal 'gift' of a half-eaten toy mouse.
